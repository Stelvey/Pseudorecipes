Usual lunch

Description

A very straightforward dish that allows some variety

Ingredients

  • Any frozen ready-to-fry meat
  • Pasta or buckwheat or rice or anything else as a side to your meat
  • Oil for the pan
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Prepare your meat, the steps are on the package
  2. Prepare your side meal, the steps are on the package
  3. Optionally, add salt while making all this
  4. Put everything on the plate and it's done
