Oatmeal

Description

It's just oatmeal

Ingredients

  • Buy some oatmeal packets that are made for brewing by hot water

Steps

  1. These things you've bought come with everything you need - even salt
  2. Just put one or two in a bowl
  3. Pour over some hot water from the kettle
  4. Wait about 5-20 minutes
  5. Mix it up and it's done
