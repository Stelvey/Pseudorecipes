Fried Vegetables

Description

Random vegetables fried and served with sour sauce

Ingredients

  • Frozen vegetables
  • Olive oil
  • Sour sauce

Steps

  1. Pour your oil on the pan
  2. Turn on the heat, keep it on low
  3. Drop your frozen vegetable mix on the pan
  4. Wait for about 15 minutes
  5. Put your fried vegetables on some plate and pour some sour sauce on top
  6. Mix it up and it's done
